SECTION 4 — COMBINING DIACRITICS & NORMALIZATION
================================================================================

Standard terminals must treat precomposed (NFC) and decomposed (NFD) strings
identically in terms of column advance (exactly 1 cell each).

NFC (Precomposed)  │ NFD (Decomposed)   │ Hex/Sequence Check
───────────────────┼────────────────────┼───────────────────────────
é (U+00E9)         │ é (U+0065 U+0301)  │ [e] + [combining acute]
Å (U+00C5)         │ Å (U+0041 U+030A)  │ [A] + [combining ring above]
ñ (U+00F1)         │ ñ (U+006E U+0303)  │ [n] + [combining tilde]
ü (U+00FC)         │ ü (U+0075 U+0308)  │ [u] + [combining diaeresis]

Combining Marks Stack (Stress/Zalgo test):
- Single:   á (a + U+0301)
- Double:  ä́ (a + U+0308 + U+0301)
- Stripped: x̸ (x + U+0338) / a̷ (a + U+0337)
- Deep Zalgo stack: Z̥̤̱͑͗͛̔͐̇̍ (Z + 10 combining marks)

Visual Alignment Check (Combining clusters MUST stay in a single column):
|a|é|ô|ñ|ü|x̸|Z͑|
|A|B |C |D |E |F |G |

Combining Marks after Punctuation / Space:
| ́|  (Space + U+0301)
|.́|  (Period + U+0301)
|-̈|  (Hyphen + U+0308)
|_̃|  (Underscore + U+0303)

- English prefix (مرحبا) English suffix
